About Me

Lynn Werre is an aspiring web programmer & instructional designer. She has a bachelors degree in Communication Sciences & Disorders, but decided to forego being a Speech Therapist in order to revolutionize the way technology is used to facilitate learning. She is a graduate student, veteran, a wife, and a mother of four who enjoys reading, rock climbing and drinking coffee.
